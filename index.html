ABOUT US

Founded in 2021 with the fundamental belief that every person deserves access to the best health care, Healink Multi Specialty Hospital is one of the preeminent healthcare organizations that offer best-in-class services and comprehensive range of treatments

Envisioned by the founders, have always believed in patient centric care through professional and ethical approach. The single-minded focus on excellence and innovative clinical care allows our premier healthcare professionals to provide advanced, innovative medical and surgical care.

Headquartered in Faridabad, India, we at Healink Multi Specialty Hospital aim at strengthening and expanding our market position by making quality health care more accessible to everyone. We constantly educate people about preventive healthcare as the key to a healthy lifestyle and overall wellness.

At Healink, we want to build ourselves as an affordable, globally-benchmarked quality-driven healthcare organisation. Our cohort of surgeons and doctors are specialists in their field and are looked upon as exemplary figures in the medical fraternity.

SERVICES

NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit)

At Healink Multi Specialty Hospital, we have a dedicated Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) to provide care for newborn babies that are premature or sick and require immediate medical assistance.

Our NICU is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and ventilators to ensure your baby gets the best care possible. We have best in class NICU service and we assure the highest level of care to the newborn babies.
